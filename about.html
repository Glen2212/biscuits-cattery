About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Biscuit's Cattery, where your feline friends are treated like royalty! Established in 2020, we have quickly become a 5-star rated cattery known for our personalized care and cozy environment.
Our founder, a lifelong cat lover, started this cattery with the vision of creating a home away from home for cats. We understand that leaving your pet can be stressful, which is why we focus on providing a nurturing and loving atmosphere. Our team is dedicated to ensuring that each cat receives individual care.

Our Mission

At Biscuit's Cattery, our mission is to provide a safe, comfortable, and loving environment for your cats while you are away.
We believe that every cat deserves the best care possible, and strive the maintain a stress free and tranquil establishment.

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team of cat lovers is here to ensure that your pets receive the best care and attention. Each member of our team is trained in feline care and is passionate about providing a home away from home for your cats.
